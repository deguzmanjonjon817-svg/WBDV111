About Our Restaurant

Tap & Silog Story

Tap & Silog started with a simple goal — to bring affordable and delicious silog meals to everyone. Built by passionate owners who love Filipino street food, we focus on quality, taste, and fast service.

From tapsilog to bangsilog, every meal is cooked fresh and served hot. Our mission is to satisfy your cravings anytime, anywhere.
